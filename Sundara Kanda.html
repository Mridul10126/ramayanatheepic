Sundara Kanda

When Hanumana was reminded his trenght he quickly started his journey to Lanka in his almighty huge form. In the way he met mountain Mainaka sent by God of Seas to help Hanumana by giving him special fruits and rest. Hanumana denied of this by saying 'No rest in Lord's work' and continued his journey. The Devas wanted to test the efficiency of Hanumana so they sent Naga Devi in the form of a demoness by the name Sursa. When they both met, Sursa told Hanumana that he should enter her mouth and become her food; Hanuman increased his size to a very mega form. Surasa also made her mouth sufficiently big to swallow Hanuman. At that time Hanuman reduced his body to a thumb size jumped in and out of Surasas’s mouth. Sursa blessed him victory. After this a Rakshasi called Simhika caught hold of his shadow and started dragging him to her mouth so that she could eat him. Hanuman again increased the size of his body and killed Simhika. On reaching Lanka, Hnaumana encountered Lanka Devi and injured her with his mighty mace(गदा). Then Hanumana met Vibhishana and got to know that Sita was in Ashoka Vatika. Hanumana then went to Ashoka Vatika and narrtaed the story of Lord Rama sitting on a tree when all demonesses were asleep. Then he conveyed Rama's message to Mother Sita and took a ring of her to make believe Lord Rama. Then Hanumana thought of doing some harm to Ravana, so he killed Prince AkshayKumara and destroyed Ashoka Vatika. He was then taken to Ravana. Ravana ordered a punishment of burning his tail. On burning the tail, Hanumana broke the chains and began burning the Lanka. Then he went back to Lord Rama along with the South contignent and informed him about Mother Sita.
