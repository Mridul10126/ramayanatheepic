Aranya-Kanda

Rama,Sita and Lakshmana spent 13 years in exile in forests. These years, while travelling, they met Sage Atri, Sage Sarbhang, Sage Valmiki and Sage Agastya before reaching Panchvati where they stayed for most of the time. While residing in Panchvati, Jayant,son of Indra, took form of crow and attacked Mother Sita to test lord's recongnition towards him. Rama recognised him and used Brahmastra on Jayant. In their 14th year of exile, a demoness Surpanakha,sister of Ravana, while visiting Panchvati, saw Rama and got attracted towards him. She asked him to m arry but Rama told he was already married to Sita and told her to ask his brother Lakshmana. Lakshmana also refused. This raged the demoness and she rushed to kill Sita. Lakshmana was quick to cut her nose with a sharp knife. Surpankha went to her brothers, Khara and Dushana. They both along with their 14,000 demons were killed in battle with Rama. One demon Akampan survived and fled to Lanka. Then Surpankha went to Ravana, demon king of Lanka. Surpankha told lie to Ravana that she went to take Sita for Ravana because she was very beautiful. Ravana raged and ordered Marich to take a form of golden deer and help him in abduction of Sita. Then in Panchvati, seeing the golden deer, Sita requests Rama to brinbg that deer. Rama wnet and told Lakshmana to stay there. Rama folows and kills Marich. But before being killed, he shouts 'Help' in the voice of Rama. Sita became worried and ordered Lakshmana to go and help Rama. Lakshmana drew a 'Lakshmana Rekha' and advised Sita not to cross it in any case. While Ravana comes in form of brahmin and asks Sita for food. Sita when give him food, Ravana abducts her in his Pushpak Vimana. Jatayu, a friend of Dashratha was residing there only and attacked Ravan to save her daughter-in-law. But he was killed by Ravana. Sita throws her ornaments so that anyone there could give them to her husband. Before dying, Jatayu told Rama about the abduction of Sita by Ravana.
